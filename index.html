My

Synopsis

I am Gilbert Mwenda, famously known as "Mwenda G" – A servant and a result-oriented leader. Ever committed to championing the rights and dignity of teachers and leadership in class. My leadership journey began in primary school as a Laudatious Prefect and has since grown through various capacities in school leadership. While in primary to high school as a YCS leader, I headed Scouts Chairman, Environmental Club, Chapel Clerk/Clerkship in Christian Union, to Dining Captain and later to numerous government school responsibilities. As a benefit, I was lucky to represent my high school in an exchange program in Scotland, UK for 10 days, a fully funded program by United Kingdom Council. Through university, I was able to serve colleagues as a Class Rep, representing my colleagues in Kiswahili Scholars Society, and as a Youth Chair in P.C.E.A Parish, served as a chair in an initiative of CBO.
